Help & Feedback
Frequently Asked Questions
How do I create a new flow?
Tap the "Build a Flow" card on the home screen, or go to Saved Flows and tap the + button. You can add moves from the library, organize them into sections, and create combinations.
What are combinations?
Combinations let you group 2-3 moves together that flow as a single sequence. Add at least 2 moves to your flow, then tap "Add Combination" to select which moves to link together.
How do entries work?
Each move starts with one entry (the default execution). Add more entries to create variations within the same move. For example, do Bridging first with parallel feet, then with externally rotated feet as a second entry.
Can I organize flows into folders?
Yes! Go to Saved Flows and tap the + dashed card to create a new folder. You can customize each folder with a color and design. Star your favorite flows and they will automatically appear in the Starred folder.
What do the warning icons mean?
Triangle warning icons appear on moves that have contraindications matching your selected client's conditions. This helps you build safe, personalized flows for each client.
How do I delete a move from my flow?
Swipe left on any move, section, or combination in the flow builder to reveal the delete button. You can also long-press a move to access more options like duplicate or combine.
